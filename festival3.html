Holi

Holi , also known as the Festival of Colours, the Festival of Spring, and the Festival of Love, is an ancient Hindu religious festival and one of the most popular festivals in Hinduism.[1][11] It celebrates the eternal and divine love of Radha Krishna.[12][13] The day also signifies the triumph of good over evil as it commemorates the victory of Lord Vishnu as Narasimha Narayana over Hiranyakashipu
